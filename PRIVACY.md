## 3. Geospatial Data Handling

Plantaah processes geospatial inputs, including polygons and related site data, for the purpose of generating analysis and reports.

We treat geospatial data as potentially sensitive because it may identify land ownership, project location, operational patterns, or user-associated activity.

We may use geospatial data to:
- Validate submitted boundaries.
- Calculate area and site characteristics.
- Run third-party environmental and mapping analyses.
- Generate downloadable reports and guides.
- Provide hosted API responses.

We do not intentionally use your geospatial inputs for unrelated advertising or unrelated profiling.
